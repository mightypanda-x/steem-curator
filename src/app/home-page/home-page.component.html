Steem Curator

Steem Curator is an effort to help the community with my knowledge of technology and steem economy. The goal of this project will always be to maximize your curation earnings by scanning through various bot activities to find best content to up-vote before major votes fall in so you earn curation reward for every vote that you cast.


Feature 1: Comments Curation
* Temporarily disabling this module to fix bugs and make it more useful.

This module looks through the current bids of 16 bots which support comment up-voting and displays the result for easy curation. I also added two links, one for steemit.com and other for busy.org. If you use busy.org, you can set voting % so you can cast more votes without depleting your voting power a lot. Even a small up-vote before the bigger votes gets you a decent share of curation pie.


Feature 2: Utopian Posts

Utopian Posts module helps in 2 different ways. One it provides for easier sorting and readability to the data on utopian site and Secondly Utopian posts are great for curation. These are posts that add value to the opensource community and most of them will get a vote from utopian guaranteeing a definite curation reward.


Feature 3: Utopian Moderator Comments

Utopian rewards its moderators by rewarding their moderators comments. Usually a comment is rewards with $7 - $9 upvote. This new feature finds the comments that will be rewarded by utopian vote. Curation results on these comments are amazingly good.


Feature 4: Oracle D Posts

Oracle D is a new project that brings investments to the steem platform and has a list of approved writers who write about these projects. These writers are rewarded with an upvote from oracle-d account. Rewards are usually in the range of $25 to $35. Since this is a lesser known whale, it provides for a great opportunity to make curation rewards.
